Journalism: The Penhouse

For my journalism module, my team and I created The Penhouse, a magazine with original journalistic pieces focusing on community and health in October 2020.














Advertising campaign: HPB iQuit campaign

For a digital marketing module, my team and I developed a comprehensive digital based marketing campaign for HPB's iQuit to engage friends and families of smokers to help smokers quit. (HBP and iQuit were not involved in this school project.)

















Media Marketing Research: COVID-19 effects on adult learning

For a media research module, my team and I conducted a study on the effects of COVID-19 on adult learning through a series of focus groups. We then analysed their responses and complied the aspects of online learning.
















Marks & Spencer PR Report

For a PR module, my team and I wrote a report detailing how M&S could improve how Gen Zs percieve them. We developed a clear PR strategy aligning M&S' values with Gen Z's values. (M&S is not involved in this school assignment in any way.)
