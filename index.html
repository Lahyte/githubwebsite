About science communication

Science communication

The dream would be to explain various topics, from the humanities and arts to the sciences, at physical and virtual events, and produce relevant video and written content. The goal would be to increase public appreciation for general and niche subjects, and to supply stakeholders with clear and accurate information for research, academic, private and public projects.

About content creation

I create explainer videos (YouTube) and discursive articles (blog). The micro-goal is to investigate subject matters, while the macro-goal is to practise science communication skills. My area of interest is academic (humanities, science, interdisciplinary), and personal projects.

Feel free to write to me regarding suggestions, requests, or discoveries.
