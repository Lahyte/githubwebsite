My time spent in school has developed me immensely. From academic to non-academic skills, my teachers has played a defining role in shaping my moral compass and my skill set.

My background in mass communication allows me to better understand the different ways to communicate effectively with my target audience. This means I can use my skills to personalise tactics in order to better educate my future students

In A CNA article, Jason Tan commentates that educators impart academic knowledge, as well as moral guidance to students. I feel a desire to make a difference in this way - to guide students in their personal and public life.

With the support of other aspiring educators and a positive attitude, I am certain I will grow into an educator worthy of shaping the talents of our future.
